Dublin Dojo

1 Upper Gardiner Street, Dublin 1

The Dublin dojo is at the northwest corner of Mountjoy Square, about a fifteen-minute walk from O’Connell bridge. The daily meditation schedule is set out below.

There is a retreat planned for the upcoming Hallowe’en Weekend. It will be held at Esker Redemptorists’ Monastery in Athenry, Co. Galway from October 28 - 31, 2016. Sleeping is dormitory style, all food is provided and will be vegetarian, and largely organic. Arrival time is Friday evening, supper will be served at 8pm, the sesshin ends on Monday after lunch
