Zendo Athenry

The Wooden House, Castlelambert, Athenry, Co. Galway

Located on a small organic farm 3 miles from Athenry and 9 miles from Galway city this Dojo provides a quiet and peaceful place to practice. Opened on 23 July 2012, it is a space for anyone interested in the practice of Zazen. People from all backgrounds are welcome.

Contact us if you have questions.
