Morning Zazen on Wednesday, 25th and Thursday 26th March, and Monday 30th and Tuesday 31st March will be **online only**. Next Wednesday and Sunday evenings are in **person only** at the dojo.

The dojo will close for the Easter break after morning zazen on Tuesday 31st March and open for zazen (online and in person) on Tuesday, April 7th.

Enjoy the Easter holidays!
